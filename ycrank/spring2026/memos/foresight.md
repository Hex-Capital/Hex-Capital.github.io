## The Idea

- **Problem:** Consumer brands in CPG, retail, and tech need to validate pricing, positioning, packaging, campaigns, and new product concepts before launch but rely on traditional panels that are slow and expensive (foresight.tt landing page).
- **Approach:** Builds AI-powered simulations of human behavior — synthetic consumers — that companies query to predict how real consumers will think, react, and choose across copy, creative, concept, conjoint/packaging, price elasticity, brand lift, and segmentation tests (foresight.tt feature list).
- **Differentiation:** Versus Quantilope (real-panel hybrid, $40M raised, 300+ enterprise clients incl. Nestle/Estee Lauder per Ditto market map 2026), Foresight is fully synthetic; versus Simile ($100M Series A, Feb 2026, Index Ventures per AI2.work) and Aaru ($50M+ Series A at $1B headline valuation, Dec 2025, Redpoint per TechCrunch), Foresight targets the consumer/CPG vertical with packaged test types (copy, conjoint, price elasticity) rather than horizontal digital-twin infrastructure (foresight.tt; askditto.io market map 2026).
- **Business Model:** [Inferred]: SaaS/subscription priced per test or seat for CPG/retail consumer-insights teams — no public pricing page exists at foresight.tt as of May 2026, and the comparable category (Quantilope, Evidenza, Ditto) ranges $22K–$100K+/year per the Ditto 2026 buyer's guide.
- **TAM/SAM:** Synthetic data market projected $1.8B (2024) → $8.2B (2029) per Ditto market map 2026; no public SAM data found for synthetic consumer-insights specifically.
- **GTM / Distribution:** [Inferred]: Direct enterprise sales to CPG/beauty/retail insights teams — Caudalie is the only named customer on foresight.tt and is cited running 15 tests/week at "25x lower cost per insight" (foresight.tt case study).

## Defensibility

- **Moat today:** No defensibility signals found in public sources — single named customer (Caudalie), no disclosed proprietary panel data, no patents found.
- **Future moat:** [Inferred]: Calibration data from enterprise customer test results (what synthetic prediction vs. real outcome was) could form a feedback loop; unproven now because only one named customer is public (foresight.tt).
- **Market structure:** [Inferred]: Traditional panel incumbents (Qualtrics, YouGov, Toluna) face revenue cannibalization moving from per-respondent panel pricing to synthetic — YouGov's response was acquiring Yabble and Toluna launched HarmonAIze as hybrid products per askditto.io market map 2026, indicating partial rather than full migration.

## Key Risks

- **Well-capitalized direct competitors:** Simile raised $100M Series A in Feb 2026 (AI2.work) and Aaru raised ~$50M at $1B headline valuation in Dec 2025 (TechCrunch) — both in the same synthetic-research category, vastly outspending a pre-seed two-person team on engineering and sales. No mitigation found beyond vertical focus on CPG.
- **Hybrid incumbent encroachment:** Quantilope ($40M, 300+ enterprise CPG clients including Estee Lauder/Nestle/Kraft Heinz per Ditto 2026) already serves Foresight's target buyer and is layering AI onto an existing real-panel relationship, creating switching-cost friction for Foresight to overcome.
- **Validity / technical-feasibility risk:** Synthetic consumer prediction accuracy versus real-world outcomes is the unresolved technical question for the entire category — public peer-reviewed validation of LLM-simulated consumer responses at the granularity Foresight sells (price elasticity, conjoint, brand lift) is not established; no Foresight-specific accuracy benchmark is published (foresight.tt).
- **Single-customer concentration in public proof:** Caudalie is the sole named customer publicly cited (foresight.tt) — case-study generalizability to broader CPG/retail/tech buyers is unverified.
- **Name disambiguation / brand collision:** "Foresight" overlaps with at least five distinct entities surfaced in search — Foresight Sports (golf), Foresight Factory (consultancy), Foresight Data ($5.5M seed, private-markets data per BusinessWire Jun 2025), Foresight Ventures (crypto), Foresight News — risking SEO, trademark, and sales-conversation friction.
