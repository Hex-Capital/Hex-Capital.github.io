# Kinect -- Sam Lessin Evaluation

Kratik Agrawal and Varun Kandula co-led Conversational Intelligence at Reevo together before founding Kinect, which is the single most interesting line in this dossier — real domain reps, not a pivot deck. That's where Kinect scores best on my card. But Kinect's category is "AI shopping agent for DTC merchants" three weeks after Shopify shipped Agentic Storefronts natively to every US merchant on Mar 24, 2026 (TechCrunch), and that's where Kinect scores worst. Right operators, wrong asset — the analytical signature is "competent execution team building on a substrate the platform owner just commoditized."

Kinect is almost a word-for-word match for the Pass Example I keep on the wall: "AI agent for [enterprise vertical], three months out of YC, four tier-1 VCs." Solana at $0.05 in 2018-2019 is the high rung on price discipline because the consensus had quit the category — the round was illegible and cheap. Kinect's round is the inverse: agentic commerce is the consensus AI thesis of 2026, McKinsey is quoting $3-5T by 2030 in the dossier itself, and Daydream already raised $50M with Forerunner and Index while Alhena is at ~$13.9M. When the dossier names McKinsey's TAM as a positive signal, the round is by definition not where I make money — the alpha was three years ago when nobody believed agents would buy things. Kinect is being underwritten at the price of an already-resolved curve.

Teamshares is the structure I'd want to see and the structure Kinect does not have. The Teamshares mechanism — employee-ownership rollup of small businesses — let me write into a vehicle no other fund could underwrite, which is exactly why the entry made sense. Kinect's dossier shows the opposite pattern: a standard YC SAFE, no pricing page, "[Inferred]: SaaS subscription with white-glove onboarding," and a competitive set where Daydream has 30x the war chest. Kinect will need five financing tacks to clear the regatta against a free Shopify-native substitute, and there is no non-standard instrument on offer that would let me underwrite Kratik and Varun as the asset instead of the cap table. This is founder-drainage in slow motion.

Reevo Conversational Intelligence plus Agrawal's Google Commerce (Hotels & Ads) plus Kandula's $200K+ contracted ARR through NextGen Consulting with Sephora and Ralph Lauren is genuine founder-market fit — the kind of resume I read carefully because it predicts they can ship and sell. Over 18 months, that compounds well on enterprise outbound and badly against a platform owner: every quarter Shopify extends Agentic Storefronts, Kinect's 10 named logos (Slumberkins, Sousa, Zupz, Ketro, ALC, Bloom, Hearthy Foods, Huppy, VapeTM, Loosey Goosey) face a free in-platform alternative their CFO can adopt with one toggle. Domain depth is a necessary input to weathering that, not sufficient — and there's no public writing from Kratik_ag or varunkand on why agents fail in DTC, which is what missionary temperament looks like at this stage.

What would have to be true for Kinect's founder-as-brand score to flip a tier? Either Kratik or Varun would need a category newsletter or a 10K-engaged-developer footprint that Kinect's catalog-feed-for-agents pitch could ride into merchant adoption — the John Fish / Bookshelved profile, where the distribution is the asset. The dossier says "count not retrievable" for both Twitter handles and no GitHub repo stars worth naming. There is no owned distribution channel here; Kinect's go-to-market is founder-led outbound to Shopify mid-market, which is exactly the channel Shopify itself can collapse with a default-on feature.

Kinect's category sits in the sleeve I publicly down-weight — "AI is just more internet" (Joe Lonsdale podcast #113, 2024). Compare to Aleo at seed, where the sleeve fit was the entire trade: pre-consensus crypto protocol with real on-chain economics, almost no generalist VC underwriting it. Kinect is the opposite vector — an LLM shell over a commerce catalog, where OpenAI ChatGPT checkout (Nov 2025), Amazon Rufus auto-buy, Perplexity shopping, and Shopify Agentic Storefronts all shipped overlapping features in the twelve months before Kinect's YC launch tweet on Apr 23, 2026. The buyer-side agents capture the conversation off-merchant, the platform owner captures the merchant-side feature, and Kinect is left selling a "brand-owned data layer" into a sandwich.

My 36-month forecast on Kinect: acqui-hired by Klaviyo, Attentive, or a mid-tier Shopify ISV for under $20M, or pivoted to merchant-side agent analytics tooling for the Shopify ecosystem, with ARR never crossing $3M before a flat bridge. The mechanism is straightforward — Shopify's Mar 24, 2026 native rollout means every quarter Kinect's wedge narrows, and a two-engineer team selling adaptive PDPs into mid-market DTC cannot out-iterate the platform that owns the merchant relationship. Kratik and Varun land somewhere good; the cap table does not.

The strongest bull case against me: Reevo Conversational Intelligence is real conversational-AI infrastructure experience, the 20% conversion lift / 14% AOV / 24% time-on-page figures (however unattributed) plus 10 logos signal that the wedge works today, and the Ro exception in my own framework says regulatory or structural complexity can justify a high-burn DTC bet even when capital efficiency is wrong. Could agentic-commerce protocol fragmentation across OpenAI, Perplexity, Amazon, and Shopify create a "50-state prescription rails" equivalent that only a neutral merchant-side layer can navigate? The dossier itself answers this: Shopify's Mar 24, 2026 rollout went to "all US merchants" simultaneously, which is the anti-Ro condition — there is no regulatory fragmentation forcing merchants toward a third-party translator. Ro applies when only VC capital can clear schleps; Kinect's schlep is a Shopify toggle. The bull case fails on its own facts. The one verifiable condition that would flip my verdict: Kinect demonstrates $500K+ retained ARR six months past Shopify's Agentic Storefronts general availability, proving merchants pay for Kinect's layer on top of the free native feature. Until then, I'm out before the meeting.
