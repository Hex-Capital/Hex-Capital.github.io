# AICE Power -- Elad Gil Evaluation

AICE Power is a catalyst-rich, miracle-poor company: the undersea-cable sabotage cycle and Pentagon Replicator-era buying did re-open the subsea market in a 24-month window, but a 2-person team that executed a 120° pivot from energy sensors to submarine swarm drones on May 16, 2026 is staring at a multi-miracle hardware build against a $60B Anduril and a $9.25B Saronic. Gr?goire Chomette and R?mi Bouteiller have shipping rhythm on paper -- the prior AICE product reached six-figure ARR per remi.bouteiller.org -- but none of that compounds into subsea autonomy, and the dossier doesn't name a single naval contact, prototype depth test, or DIU/SBIR award. That high-catalyst / low-miracle-architecture pairing is the signature, and in my experience it produces "interesting market, wrong vehicle" outcomes.

The **Anduril** (2022) Series D is the natural HIGH rung on the market-catalyst ladder, and AICE Power genuinely shares the surface mechanism -- naval/coastal defense forecast at 41.24% of the UUV market in 2026 (Globe Newswire May 2026), undersea cable incidents 2023-2025, Replicator pulling DoD toward distributed attritable systems. That's a real re-opening, not a TAM slide. Where AICE Power diverges from the Anduril template is that the Anduril catalyst arrived attached to Trae Stephens' Palantir-forward-deployed muscle and Palmer Luckey's Oculus shipping history; AICE Power has Chomette's MIT AeroAstro thesis on jet-engine deep learning and Bouteiller's wearable-hot-flash predictor at Amira Health. Same wave, no surfboard built for this water -- I'd score the catalyst genuinely but discount the team's ability to monetize it inside the window.

**Lyft** Series C is the LOW rung on the index-position axis -- a #2 in a network-effect market where capturing share against the leader is the entire business model, and the dossier doesn't show inversion economics. That failure mechanism shows up cleanly in AICE Power: subsea swarms aren't an index on US defense modernization the way Anduril's Lattice is -- AICE Power has to **win against** Anduril's Dive-LD/Dive-XL and Saronic's surface platforms on the same DIU/Navy procurement tracks, not ride them. There's no rail, no developer channel, no workflow layer every other entrant has to touch. "Swarm modular subsea" is a product positioning, not a distribution position, and at 2 people with zero DoD relationships disclosed AICE Power is structurally the Lyft-shaped bet in a Stripe-shaped market.

On revenue velocity -- the dimension that compounds or fails over 18 months -- AICE Power starts from zero on the current product, with the only ARR signal ("six figures") tied to the pre-pivot energy-sensor business that no longer exists. Defense hardware procurement compounds slowly even when it's working; Anduril's Feb 2024 DIU contract didn't hit CAMP demo until 2026. If AICE Power hasn't signed a sub-$5M SBIR Phase II or DIU OTA by mid-2027, the curve doesn't exist to compound from, and "something's fundamentally broken" is the Tim Ferriss Show #863 (April 2026) line that applies. The pivot date of May 16, 2026 means the clock started 11 days before YC batch -- this is not a curve, this is a thesis statement.

On founder execution rhythm, the counterfactual is sharp: for AICE Power to flip a tier on this dimension, the dossier would have to show a working subsea prototype demoed in a US Navy test pool, OR a named DIU program manager engaged on a sole-source path, OR a hardware iteration cadence visible on GitHub or in test footage -- none of which appear. Bouteiller's @rbouteiller GitHub has "no notable repo found," Chomette has no public Twitter, and the Perplexity-Aravind pattern of "showing up a week later with the thing we discussed built" is exactly the artifact missing here. The prior six-figure ARR is a real positive signal that AICE Power's founders can build *something*, but the something they built was a building-energy sensor, which transfers neither domain expertise nor procurement relationships.

The single-miracle test is where AICE Power most clearly fails my framework -- **Color Genomics** (2015) is the parallel: I co-founded it explicitly as a workflow layer on commodity sequencing precisely so the bet was *not* multi-miracle. AICE Power needs (a) subsea swarm autonomy with acoustic comms, (b) ruggedized hardware reliability at 100-600m depth, (c) DoD procurement penetration as a 2-person team with no cleared personnel, and (d) France-US ITAR compliance on hardware crossing borders. That's four independent miracles, and per NFX's *Unusual Signs* the compounded probability is where these deals die. Anduril cleared its exception by isolating the miracle to software-on-existing-autonomy; AICE Power has not architected an equivalent isolation.

My 36-month forecast: AICE Power either dies during a 2027 seed-extension raise unable to show a working prototype + named DoD customer, or pivots to selling a swarm-coordination software module *into* Anduril, Saronic, or HavocAI as a component supplier with sub-$500K revenue. No outcome above $5M ARR, no DIU OTA above $3M, no acquisition above acqui-hire pricing. The mechanism is the multi-miracle compound -- each of the four miracles has maybe 30% odds in 36 months for this team, and 0.3^4 is the math that produces the modal outcome.

The strongest bull case against my pass leans on the Anduril Known Exception: the catalyst is real, swarm-attritable is exactly what Replicator wants and what Anduril's monolithic Dive-LD is *not*, and a French manufacturing base could produce iteration speed below US defense-prime cost curves. The bull says I'm pattern-matching team weakness when the market structure (distributed cheap subsea) explicitly *rewards* small teams over primes. That sting lands -- but it breaks on the dossier: AICE Power has no prototype, no DoD relationship, no cleared personnel, and pivoted 11 days before YC batch, which means the Replicator-fit thesis is a Spring 2026 hypothesis, not a shipped position. The verifiable condition that would flip my verdict is a signed DIU OTA or Navy SBIR Phase II above $1M with a demonstrated subsea prototype by Q2 2027 -- absent that, AICE Power is the Lyft-shaped bet in the Anduril-shaped catalyst, and I pass.
