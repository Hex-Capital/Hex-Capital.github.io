# Refortifai -- Vinod Khosla Evaluation

Refortifai is a tool-layer play sitting inside a stack I already own through **Astrus** (Khosla Ventures led the $8M USD seed, Oct 2025) — and that single fact reframes everything else in the dossier. The dimensional signature here is "instrumentally important consequence, structurally wrong check size" — analog circuit design is genuinely a chokepoint for the physical-AI future I have been writing checks into since **Physical Intelligence** (2024), but Refortifai's $5-15M capital profile and three-engineer team is not the shape of a bet I write directly when I am already exposed through Astrus's RL-for-layout focus.

On consequence, the load-bearing analogy is **DoorDash** (2013), not **OpenAI** (2019) — Refortifai does not collapse an incumbent category, it accelerates the substrate beneath one. Every robot, every sensor, every power converter that underwrites the "$15 trillion of U.S. GDP is labor" frame (Fortune 2026-03-06) bottlenecks on analog and mixed-signal silicon that EE Times (Apr 2026) calls "historically resistant to automation." Where Refortifai diverges from the DoorDash analogy is that DoorDash reshaped a consumer flow end-to-end; Refortifai reshapes a tool used by ~9,000 analog designers, with the gain captured by chip teams downstream. That is real but instrumental — the success-state at maximum ambition is "Synopsys-and-Cadence-but-faster," not "the analog design profession ceases to exist as a discipline." Cognichip's stated 50% timeline / 75% cost reduction (Businesswire, Apr 2026) is the honest ceiling, and a 50% gain is closer to my Pass Example than to my Impossible Foods rung.

The capital-fit ladder is where Refortifai fails the structural test most starkly — when a $5M seed clears the technical milestone, I am the wrong investor. **Commonwealth Fusion** (2019) needed a $115M Series A because tokamak physics demands plant-scale capital that no consensus VC will touch; **Cognichip** raised $60M for a physics-informed foundation model spanning digital, analog, and mixed-signal because the training compute is real. Refortifai's nearest comp Astrus closed at $8M USD (Fasken, Oct 2025) for the same RL-for-analog premise, and Silimate (YC S23) and Quilter ($10M Benchmark Series A) cluster in the same band. Three engineers building RL environments on top of commodity foundation models do not need a Khosla-scale check, which means any Tier-1 generalist can fund this — and the dossier's commoditization-risk note that "multiple well-funded entrants already exist" is the same observation in different words.

On technical risk, Refortifai compounds over 18 months only if the RL-environment artifact accumulates as proprietary benchmark data faster than Cognichip's 30-customer engagement loop generates the same training signal. Atman Kar's RTL design experience at Texas Instruments and Tenstorrent (LinkedIn) is digital, not analog — the very EE Times article describing analog as resistant to automation explains why "RL approaches that work in digital flows may not transfer." There is no released paper, no public benchmark, no Refortifai artifact yet to falsify the miracle, and the website returns HTTP 403. That is not technical risk that repels consensus VCs; it is technical ambiguity that repels everyone equally.

On founder defiance of expert consensus, the score would flip a tier only if CEO Sayan Mitra's background turned out to include a publicly defended contrarian position on analog automation — a paper, a thesis, a rejected proposal at TI or Cadence that they are now operationalizing. The dossier records the opposite: "No public documentation of CEO Sayan Mitra's analog-circuit or semiconductor background was retrieved." My TED Talk pointed at the UC Berkeley 28,000-prediction study because experts extrapolate the past; the Refortifai founding team is junior domain practitioners extrapolating their last job, which is the soft-negative profile, not the consensus-defying one.

On founder learning rate, the parallel is the **Slash** (2024) Kanye pivot story — that documented thesis-update is the one signal that made me override a weak consequence-magnitude prior at $100M Series C. Refortifai's dossier records no pivot, no public thesis change, no prior sold company, no "we were wrong about X" moment. Absent that, the Slash override does not apply, and the YC pedigree alone is not sufficient — Slash had pedigree AND a pivot story.

The 36-month forecast: Refortifai is acqui-hired by Synopsys, Cadence, or Tenstorrent for under $50M, or quietly pivots toward digital RTL agents where CTO Atman Kar's actual TI/Tenstorrent expertise lives, or shuts down as Cognichip's 30-customer wedge and Astrus's Khosla-funded layout focus compress the niche from both sides. The mechanism is straightforward — incumbents Synopsys and Cadence already ship Cadence.AI and Virtuoso Studio agentic workflows inside the existing flow, the analog-EDA budget is held by ~30 procurement orgs globally, and Refortifai has no warm-channel disclosure beyond Atman Kar's TI alumni status.

The strongest bull case against my pass: Refortifai's positioning as "build + debug + automate" spans more of the design loop than Astrus's layout-only focus, which means inside my own portfolio Astrus and Refortifai are complements, not substitutes — and the right move is to fund both and let them merge in 18 months. The dismantling: that bull case requires Refortifai to demonstrate the multi-agent loop is technically real, and the dossier offers zero artifact — no paper, no benchmark, no demo, no customer — only a 403 website and an "AI Agents for Analog Circuit Design" tagline. I do not write Khosla checks on taglines that any of the six other YC analog-EDA entrants in the next batch could write identically. The verdict flips to Invest if Refortifai publishes a falsifiable benchmark showing their RL environment closes a specific analog design task (e.g., DC-DC buck converter sizing, the same problem Celera claims 10x on) faster than a Cadence.AI baseline by a published margin — that single artifact would move it onto the **Genesis AI** (2025) ladder where I co-led $105M because the demo was real.
