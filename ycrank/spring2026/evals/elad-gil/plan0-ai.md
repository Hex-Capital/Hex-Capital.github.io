# PLAN0 AI -- Elad Gil Evaluation

Arash Barati's $7B CRE PE/lending/development rolodex is the most thesis-relevant fact in the entire PLAN0 AI dossier, and yet PLAN0 AI's defining signature is the inverse of what I fund: a structurally clean single-miracle architecture sold by a credentialed-but-not-shipping team into a category with no named 24-month catalyst and no disclosed revenue curve. PLAN0 AI looks fundable; it doesn't look like an index on a re-opening.

**Harvey** (2023) is the HIGH rung on my revenue-velocity ladder -- $3B → $5B → $8B in months, the canonical "just working" signature (TechCrunch, Nov 2025). The mechanism that makes Harvey load-bearing here is that PLAN0 AI is pitching the same buyer archetype Harvey hit (enterprise professionals with capital-markets workflows), so a real curve would be just as legible. Instead PLAN0 AI offers "$20B of projects already running through the platform" -- a throughput vanity metric, not ARR, not MoM, not a named lender logo signed in the last 90 days. PLAN0 AI's site is demo-gated with no pricing page (plan0.ai, 2026); StartupHub.ai lists $4M raised at Seed with team size 5 and zero customer references. In the AI cycle, silence on growth is itself a signal because real growth is trivially disclosable -- PLAN0 AI is either pre-revenue or strategically opaque, and at angel pricing neither pencils against the Harvey template.

The **Lyft** Series C pass mechanism was that I scored a clear #2 in a winner-take-all market, and the dossier showed no share-inversion path (20VC interview). That failure mode partially rhymes here: PLAN0 AI is the #2-or-#3 by capitalization against Togal.AI's $17.2M raise (Crunchbase via search snippet, Aug 2025), with Togal hitting 98% floor-plan accuracy at $299/mo per user (Togal blog, 2026). The asymmetry softens only if you accept PLAN0 AI's wedge claim -- developer/lender underwriting vs. contractor takeoff -- as a genuine market segmentation rather than positioning. PLAN0 AI's dossier doesn't disclose a single named lender or developer customer, doesn't quantify the partnership count behind the "localized cost database," and doesn't benchmark accuracy publicly the way InEight (1.8% miss rate) or Togal (98%) do. Without that, PLAN0 AI is a less-funded clone in a category with declared competitors, which is the Lyft Series C pattern dressed in construction-tech clothing.

Market catalyst: PLAN0 AI's dossier names "CV models reaching production-grade plan-parsing accuracy in 2025-2026" with at least 6 platforms already benchmarked (Robotics & Automation News, Feb 2026). Mechanism analysis: a catalyst that produces six benchmarked competitors in eighteen months is not a re-opening for PLAN0 AI -- it's commoditization arriving on schedule. The Stripe-2010 / Coinbase-2013 / Anduril-2022 pattern requires that peers misread the market as dead; PLAN0 AI is entering a market that peers correctly read as opening, with capital already deployed. That compounds against PLAN0 AI over 18 months because the cost-database differentiation is licensable from RSMeans or Gordian by any well-capitalized incumbent.

Founder shipping rhythm: PLAN0 AI's CTO Shervin Barati was an Apple engineering manager on AirPods hearing-health features -- shipped at scale, but as a manager inside the world's most resourced product org, which is not the **Perplexity** (2022) Aravind-Srinivas-builds-it-between-meetings signal. Counterfactual: for PLAN0 AI to flip a tier on this dimension, the dossier would need named independent artifacts -- a side project, a GitHub, a prior 0-to-1 product the brothers shipped before raising the $4M. None of that exists in any source reviewed. Arash Barati's MSRED + $7B underwriting experience is founder-market fit on the *buyer* side, not the *builder* side, and PLAN0 AI is pre-seed where shipping is the only legible signal.

**Decagon** (2023) is the single-anchor parallel for PLAN0 AI's single-miracle structure -- one validated capability (LLM customer support quality), remaining work is GTM and product. PLAN0 AI is architected similarly: the CV plan-parsing miracle is mostly solved (six benchmarked platforms prove this), and PLAN0 AI's remaining bets are the cost-database aggregation and the developer/lender GTM motion -- both execution, neither science. This is PLAN0 AI's cleanest dimension, and it's why I'm not at Strong Pass. The risk is that PLAN0 AI's "single miracle" is the wrong miracle -- the value isn't 30-minute turnaround, it's accuracy on heterogeneous plans, which the dossier conspicuously does not benchmark.

36-month forecast: PLAN0 AI reaches $2-6M ARR in CRE developer/lender underwriting, then gets acquired by Procore, Autodesk Construction Cloud, or a cost-data incumbent (RSMeans/Gordian) for $40-120M as an acqui-hire-plus-product tuck-in -- not a venture-scale outcome from my seat. The mechanism: Arash Barati's relationships convert into 10-20 enterprise pilots, the product wedges into capital-side workflows that Togal can't reach because of contractor channel conflict, but PLAN0 AI never inverts Togal's funding lead or builds an index position because cost data is licensable and the "$20B throughput" claim doesn't translate to defensible ARR.

The strongest bull case against the forecast is that PLAN0 AI is **Color Genomics** (2015) in CRE drag: workflow infrastructure layered on a hard-tech commodity (CV plan parsing replaces sequencing), with a domain-native CEO who can credibly sell Blackstone Real Estate and Brookfield in a way no contractor-takeoff vendor can. If Arash Barati's $7B network converts into ten named lender logos and PLAN0 AI becomes the underwriting-side standard while Togal stays stuck contractor-side, the bifurcation is real and PLAN0 AI compounds as an index on CRE underwriting modernization. That sting matters because the Known Exception template fits structurally. It loses on dossier evidence: Color was Elad-operated, and PLAN0 AI's "$20B of projects" claim is unverifiable promoter-language with no named counterparty, no ARR, no accuracy benchmark, no pricing page -- the exact "false signal vs. just working" distinction (TechCrunch, Nov 2025) that I use to filter pilot-tourism from buying behavior. One verifiable condition flips this: PLAN0 AI discloses >20% MoM revenue growth with two named top-20 CRE lender or developer customers within 90 days, at which point I reopen at any reasonable mark.
