## The Idea

- **Problem:** Product/CX teams "force feedback into categories" using outdated systems and cannot identify root causes for churn, failed activation, or checkout abandonment (pothlabs.com, 2026).
- **Approach:** Agentic platform connects to company data sources (feedback, employee schedules, inventory, supplier deliveries), auto-generates hypotheses, and validates them via adaptive user surveys with statistical confidence metrics (pothlabs.com, 2026).
- **Differentiation:** Dovetail organizes/transcribes qualitative research, Sprig fires in-app surveys at behavioral triggers, Maze runs structured usability tests (dovetail.com; sprig.com; maze.co); [Inferred]: Poth's differentiator is autonomous hypothesis generation + adaptive survey validation rather than human-driven tagging or static survey templates (pothlabs.com).
- **Business Model:** No pricing page is published; site only exposes "Book a demo" CTAs (pothlabs.com, 2026); [Inferred]: enterprise/contact-sales SaaS motion targeting product and CX teams.
- **TAM/SAM:** Customer feedback software market $2.26B in 2026 at 13.2% CAGR (Global Growth Insights, 2026); broader customer analytics market $10.46B in 2024 projected $38.08B by 2032 (Verified Market Research, 2026); company positions itself against a "$10B feedback industry" (pothlabs.com, 2026).
- **GTM / Distribution:** Demo-request funnel via pothlabs.com/demo and pothlabs.com/contact, with offices listed in "NY/SF" (pothlabs.com/contact, 2026); [Inferred]: founder-led outbound to product/CX leaders, leveraging YC S26 network.
