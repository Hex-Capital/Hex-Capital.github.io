## The Idea

- **Problem:** Short-term rental operators stall between 10-50 listings because operational complexity scales non-linearly across "5 to 10 disconnected tools," causing review and rate decay (YC Launch page, 2026).
- **Approach:** Unifies communications, tasks, workforce, smart homes, bookings, accounting, and revenue management into a single data layer with 200+ integrations (Airbnb, VRBO, Guesty, Breezeway, etc.) so AI agents can orchestrate guest journeys, turnovers, claims, and pricing end-to-end (trellistech.com, 2026).
- **Differentiation:** Vs. Guesty/Hostaway (PMS systems with bolt-on automations) and Breezeway (cleaning/inspection ops) — Trellis positions as an AI-native OS that "builds your software" dynamically per operator rather than a fixed-feature PMS (Lodovico Benvenuti quote, YC page).
- **Business Model:** "Get started for free" CTA on landing page; no public per-seat or per-unit pricing tiers disclosed (trellistech.com, 2026). [Inferred]: Likely per-listing SaaS given STR competitor norms.
- **TAM/SAM:** No public TAM/SAM data found for this specific segment; competitor scale benchmark — Guesty valued at $900M at Series F (TechCrunch, Apr 2024); Hostaway valued at $925M (TechCrunch, Dec 2024).
- **GTM / Distribution:** [Inferred]: Founder Jan Sahagun's STR Tech Report media platform, 20+ industry events attended, and existing relationships with "thousands of property managers" function as direct distribution (YC Launch page, 2026).

## Defensibility

- **Moat today:** 200+ live integrations across the STR tool stack (trellistech.com, 2026); [Inferred]: integration breadth raises switching cost relative to a new entrant rebuilding the same connectors.
- **Future moat:** [Inferred]: Cross-customer learning network ("every SOP that survives contact with guests… feeds back into the network") could compound into a data moat, but unproven at 6-person stage with no disclosed customer count (company description).
- **Market structure:** [Inferred]: Incumbents Guesty ($409M raised) and Hostaway ($543M raised) are PE-backed at ~$900M+ valuations (TechCrunch, 2024) — rebuilding around AI agents would risk cannibalizing their per-seat SaaS revenue model.
- **Commoditization risk:** Founder Lodovico Benvenuti states the team "reached feature parity with competitors in two weeks" using AI (YC page, 2026), implying low barriers to feature replication by other AI-native entrants including his prior employer HostAI/Conduit (YC W24).
