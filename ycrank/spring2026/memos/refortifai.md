## The Idea

- **Problem:** Analog circuit design remains heavily manual and reliant on expert intuition, with EE Times (Apr 2026) noting analog/mixed-signal domains have been "historically resistant to automation," and Cognichip stating design timelines can be cut 50% and cost 75% with automation (Businesswire, Apr 2026).
- **Approach:** "AI Agents to build, debug and automate analog circuit design" using "RL environments to solve and evaluate complex tasks involving electronic design" (YC page, 2026).
- **Differentiation:** Astrus (Toronto) automates analog *layout* with RL/deep learning (BetaKit, Oct 2025); Cognichip uses a physics-informed foundation model across digital/analog/mixed-signal (SiliconANGLE, Apr 2026); Celera focuses on autonomous analog IC sampling via ChipHub (EE News Europe); [Inferred]: Refortifai's positioning as multi-agent "build + debug + automate" spans more of the design loop than Astrus's layout focus, but no public product detail confirms the specific scope.
- **Business Model:** No public data found; website https://refortif.ai returned HTTP 403 at time of research.
- **TAM/SAM:** Global EDA tools market $19.22B in 2025 → $28.85B by 2030 at 8.46% CAGR (Mordor Intelligence); global analog & mixed-signal device market $66.54B in 2025 (Global Growth Insights). No public analog-EDA-specific TAM found.
- **GTM / Distribution:** [Inferred]: Direct enterprise sales to semiconductor design teams, given the YC "B2B / Semiconductors" tags (YC page) and the CTO's prior employer Texas Instruments (LinkedIn) as a likely warm-intro channel.
