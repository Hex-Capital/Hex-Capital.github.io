## The Idea

- **Problem:** AI-native enterprises lack a single source of operational context — written knowledge sits scattered across documentation, dashboards, tickets, CRM fields, and policies, while procedures and judgment calls remain undocumented (heysavant.com).
- **Approach:** Savant plugs into existing tools (Google Workspace, Office 365, Salesforce, Slack, Zendesk, Intercom, custom databases) to consolidate written knowledge, capture unwritten procedures/exceptions, and serve them as context to employees and AI agents at decision time (heysavant.com).
- **Differentiation:** [Inferred]: Versus Glean (enterprise search across 100+ apps; $300M ARR, TechCrunch May 2026) the positioning emphasizes capturing undocumented procedures rather than indexing existing documents; versus Guru (verified-truth wiki/agent platform, getguru.com) the pitch targets AI agents as a first-class consumer of the knowledge layer; versus Dust (multiplayer enterprise AI, SiliconANGLE May 2026) Savant is framed as a memory/context store rather than an agent-building workspace.
- **Business Model:** No public data found on pricing — heysavant.com displays no pricing page. [Inferred]: Per-seat or consumption-based enterprise SaaS, consistent with the integration-heavy enterprise category.
- **TAM/SAM:** AI in Knowledge Management market valued at >$5B in 2026 with double-digit CAGR through 2030 (market.us); broader Knowledge Management Software market $15.92B in 2026 (Straits Research) to $26.4B in 2026 (Fortune Business Insights).
- **GTM / Distribution:** No public GTM data found. [Inferred]: Direct founder-led enterprise sales to AI-native companies, given pre-launch stage and 2-person team.

## Key Risks

- **Direct competition from well-funded incumbents:** Glean ($300M ARR, $7.2B valuation, TechCrunch May 2026) and Dust ($60M+ raised, 3,000+ org customers, SiliconANGLE May 2026) target the same enterprise integration surface (Slack, Salesforce, Google Workspace, Office 365) listed on heysavant.com.
- **Platform-vendor substitution:** Microsoft 365 Copilot Agents, Atlassian Rovo, and Notion Custom Agents bundle agent/knowledge functionality into the same SaaS suites Savant integrates with (Coworker AI 2026), creating bundling risk for a standalone memory layer.
- **No disclosed customers or revenue:** heysavant.com shows no customer logos, pricing page, or traction metrics; no press coverage, Product Hunt listing, or social-media presence surfaced in search — validation signals beyond YC backing are absent.
- **Capture of undocumented knowledge is technically unproven:** The product's stated mechanism — extracting "procedures, exceptions, judgment calls, and unwritten rules" (heysavant.com) — is not demonstrated by any public benchmark, case study, or customer testimonial.
