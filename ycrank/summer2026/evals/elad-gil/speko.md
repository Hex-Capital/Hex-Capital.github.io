# Speko -- Elad Gil Evaluation

Beknazar Abdikamalov's Speko is the cleanest single-miracle architecture I've seen this batch mounted onto the weakest revenue-velocity disclosure -- a routing gateway that requires only "the market keeps splintering across STT/LLM/TTS providers" to be technically correct, published against a dossier that reports zero customers, zero ARR, and zero named logos while **Inworld Router** already sits at $117M raised and $0-markup pricing (BusinessWire, Jun 2026). That pairing tells me exactly what Speko is: a thesis-clean infrastructure bet arriving 12-18 months after the obvious version of itself got funded to Series B.

**Decagon** (2023) is the shape Speko wants to be on the single-miracle axis -- one validated capability (LLMs handle CX turns), and the remaining work is GTM and SDK ergonomics. Speko rhymes: the "miracle" is that per-provider voice performance varies enough across language/latency/cost that routing outperforms picking one, and the rest is developer distribution. No new physics, no regulatory unlock required, no chemistry risk -- Beknazar just has to ship a benchmark harness and a routing SLA. Where Speko diverges from **Decagon** is that Decagon entered a wide-open workflow (customer support) in my *AI Market Clarity* "still open" bucket, whereas Speko is entering a category where **Inworld Router**'s $117M and **OpenRouter**'s newly-added TTS/STT endpoints (openrouter.ai docs) have already declared the routing-gateway pattern is worth capitalizing. Clean single miracle, crowded landing zone.

**Harvey** (2023) is the anti-analog on revenue velocity -- "$3B ? $5B ? $8B in months" is what "just working" looks like in dollars (TechCrunch, Nov 2025), and the dossier for Speko contains no MoM revenue figure, no signed enterprise logo, no pilot count, no waitlist number. The Speko dossier lists ~340ms median turn latency and 0.76ms retrieval across 100K documents as headline traction -- both self-reported on speko.ai with no third-party benchmark. In the AI cycle, silence on growth *is* the signal: real growth is easy to disclose, and Beknazar has instead disclosed engineering metrics that don't have buyers attached. If Speko had a $40K MRR line running 30% MoM against a named voice-agent customer, it would be in the dossier. It is not.

Speko's catalyst story is where the framework gets ruthless. My **Anduril** (2022), **Stripe** (2010), and **Coinbase** (2013) entries each named a specific 24-month re-opening -- Ukraine, PayPal-era merchant-account friction, post-skepticism institutional crypto curiosity. Speko's dossier gives me "[Inferred]: Proliferation of specialized voice providers... crossed a threshold in 2025-2026 where per-language performance varies enough that routing outperforms single-provider choice." That's not a catalyst; that's a market-structure observation. **Inworld** raising $117M at $500M and **Vapi** winning Amazon Ring at ~$500M (TechCrunch, May 2026) is the actual catalyst evidence -- and it points at the fact that the routing/orchestration window opened 12-18 months ago and was funded. Speko is skating to where the puck already was.

On index position, the counterfactual: for Speko's score to flip into the "Stripe-style index" tier, the dossier would need to show either exclusive provider terms (Deepgram or ElevenLabs routing preferentially through Speko), a proprietary benchmarking dataset no competitor could replicate, or LiveKit-style embedded distribution where Speko becomes the default gateway inside a fast-growing runtime. Beknazar has shipped a LiveKit adapter, which is directionally right -- but an adapter is not a distribution position, it's a checkbox. Meanwhile **Bland AI** ($100M+ Series C) and **Vapi** ($72M) are vertically integrating the stack, meaning the buyers most likely to need routing are being courted by platforms that eliminate the need for routing. Speko is a neutral gateway in a market whose buyers are being trained to buy bundled agents.

Beknazar's founder-shipping signal is the one paragraph where Speko earns real credit. Compared to my **Perplexity** (2022) pattern -- Aravind pinging on LinkedIn and showing up a week later with the thing built -- Beknazar has publicly shipped a TypeScript SDK (@spekoai/sdk), a Python SDK (spekoai), an MCP server with OAuth, a LiveKit adapter, and previously co-founded **Hupo** to a $4M raise from DST Global, Meta, Goodwater, and Collaborative Fund (Pivot.uz; balancethegrind.co). That's not a promoter, that's a shipper who has previously convinced institutional capital. The concern is that YC lists Speko as a solo founder for a 4-person team -- the *High Growth Handbook* (2018) lens says that structure breaks somewhere around headcount 15-25 unless a CTO or CRO is named, and no such person surfaces publicly.

My 36-month forecast on Speko: absorbed as a sub-$50M acqui-hire into **Vapi**, **Retell**, or **LiveKit** by Q3 2028, with disclosed ARR sub-$2M at exit -- the routing-gateway thesis is correct, but **Inworld Router**'s $117M war chest at $0-markup pricing compresses Speko's margin below sustainability before Speko can build a distribution moat, and the voice-agent platforms accrete the routing logic in-house rather than paying a middleware layer. Beknazar lands somewhere valuable after; Speko-the-company does not become the index.

The strongest bull case against my pass: **OpenRouter** itself was a solo-founder-led text routing gateway that compounded on developer distribution while the well-funded competitors flailed, and Beknazar has already shipped the SDK-plus-MCP-plus-LiveKit surface area that historically wins developer routing. If Speko posts a named enterprise voice-agent customer with disclosed >20% MoM revenue growth in the next 90 days -- the **Harvey** signature -- I flip to invest, because that would prove the "false signal vs. just working" test (TechCrunch, Nov 2025) has resolved on the "just working" side and the crowded competitor field becomes evidence of thesis validation rather than margin compression. Absent that specific disclosure, the Speko dossier reads as clean thesis with no curve, and clean-thesis-no-curve in the 2026 AI cycle is exactly the pattern I've been trained to pass on.
