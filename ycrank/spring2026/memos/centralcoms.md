## The Idea

- **Problem:** Property managers lose tickets, miss tenant/vendor follow-ups, and stall operations across maintenance and leasing workflows currently handled inside AppFolio, Buildium, and Yardi (YC page, company description).
- **Approach:** AI agents that operate directly inside AppFolio, Buildium, and Yardi to track maintenance calls, chase open tickets, and update owners/tenants/vendors with multi-step workflow resolution (YC page).
- **Differentiation:** [Inferred]: Versus EliseAI (leasing-focused chatbot, $100M ARR per SiliconANGLE Aug 2025) CentralComs targets full maintenance ticket lifecycle; versus AppFolio Realm-X and Buildium Lumina AI (vendor-native AI, AppFolio Blog 2026) it operates as an external agent layer across multiple PMS platforms; versus Rentvine Fixie (native maintenance AI, Showdigs review 2026) it is cross-platform rather than single-stack.
- **Business Model:** No public data found on pricing; [Inferred]: B2B SaaS to property management firms, likely per-unit or per-seat given PMS-integration model.
- **TAM/SAM:** No public TAM/SAM data found for this specific segment; comparable EliseAI cites 10% of U.S. apartment market penetration at $100M ARR (SiliconANGLE, Aug 2025).
- **GTM / Distribution:** No public GTM data found; [Inferred]: Direct sales to SMB/mid-market property managers via PMS integrations (AppFolio/Buildium/Yardi) named on YC page.
