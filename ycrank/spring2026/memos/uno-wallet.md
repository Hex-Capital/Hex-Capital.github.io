## The Idea

- **Problem:** Cardholders either guess which credit card to use at checkout or manually juggle rotating rewards categories, leaving points/miles/cashback unredeemed (YC company description; myunowallet.com).
- **Approach:** A mobile wallet performs real-time merchant-and-category detection to route a tap-to-pay transaction to the cardholder's highest-earning card, activated via a power-button double-click on the phone (myunowallet.com).
- **Differentiation:** Kudos and MaxRewards operate primarily as online-checkout browser extensions/recommendation apps, while Uno is positioned as an Apple Wallet–style mobile wallet for in-person tap payments (myunowallet.com FAQ; joinkudos.com; maxrewards.com).
- **Business Model:** No public data found on pricing; [Inferred]: Likely freemium subscription consistent with Kudos/MaxRewards positioning, given Uno is in private beta with no published pricing page (myunowallet.com).
- **TAM/SAM:** No public TAM/SAM data found for this specific segment.
- **GTM / Distribution:** Waitlist-gated private beta with "Request Access" CTA, plus active hiring of growth/social roles at university career boards (myunowallet.com; careers.tufts.edu; capd.mit.edu).

## Defensibility

- **Moat today:** 20,456-person waitlist (myunowallet.com) as a pre-launch demand signal; no other public moat signals found.
- **Future moat:** [Inferred]: Personal-spending data accumulated across users could improve card recommendations and switching costs, but unproven absent a launched product.
- **Market structure:** [Inferred]: Card issuers (Chase, Amex) cannot natively recommend competitors' cards without channel conflict, leaving room for a neutral aggregator (myunowallet.com positioning).
- **Commoditization risk:** Multiple funded competitors (Kudos $17.2M total, MaxRewards 900K members) already execute card-picking via web extensions and apps, indicating the underlying logic is replicable (techcrunch.com 2024-05-17; maxrewards.com).

## Key Risks

- **Funded incumbent crowding:** Kudos has raised $17.2M and reports 200K members, and MaxRewards reports 900K members, both occupying the "AI picks the best card" positioning Uno is launching into (techcrunch.com 2024-05-17; maxrewards.com); no mitigation publicly disclosed.
- **Platform/NFC dependency:** A double-click-to-pay mobile wallet relies on iOS NFC/Wallet APIs and tap-to-pay rails; Apple historically restricts third-party NFC payments, which could constrain the core "pick at checkout" UX (myunowallet.com product description); no public mitigation found.
- **Pre-revenue, pre-launch execution risk:** Product is in private beta with Q2 2026 public launch referenced in hiring posts (capd.mit.edu via search snippet) and no public revenue, paying users, or app-store presence found.
- **Founder name disambiguation:** Multiple individuals named "Saket Joshi" appear in search; this dossier ties the YC co-founder to the xAI/Harvard MS Data Science profile based on the YC page's "xAI" credential reference and the matching San Francisco/Harvard location (search snippets).
- **Issuer/affiliate revenue dependency:** [Inferred]: Comparable apps (Kudos, MaxRewards) rely on card-application affiliate revenue and optional subscriptions; this channel is exposed to issuer policy changes and CFPB scrutiny of credit-card marketing (industry context; no Uno-specific disclosure found).
