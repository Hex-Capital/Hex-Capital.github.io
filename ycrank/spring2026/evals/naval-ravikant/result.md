# Result -- Naval Ravikant Evaluation

Savio Martin shipped Codehouse to 961 GitHub stars and won Product Hunt Maker of the Year 2021 before he could legally drink in California, which makes Result a high founder-media / low network-architecture company — the CTO is the leverage, the product is not. That pairing is rare and instructive: I'm being asked to underwrite Savio's distribution against a bundle (incorporation + banking + payments + agents) where every underlying rail is permissioned by someone else's charter. Aaryan Kushwah is 17 and CEO, Savio is 19 and CTO, and the wrapper around the work is Mercury's BaaS partner, Stripe's PSP, Delaware's franchise tax authority, and whoever provides the agent inference. The founders are code-leverage natives; Result the product is not.

Twitter (2007) is the HIGH rung on permissionless leverage — anyone posts, zero marginal cost, no gatekeeper between Dorsey and a user. The shared mechanism I want from Result is the same: a code-or-media surface that compounds Savio's 50.5K X following @saviomartin7 into product-led adoption without asking anyone permission. Where Result diverges is the substrate. The dossier names no BaaS partner on result.dev as of May 2026, but you cannot ship "incorporation + accounts + payments" without one, and the moment Result depends on a Mercury or a Stripe to clear money, the architecture has flipped from permissionless to permissioned-as-feature. Savio's media leverage is real and Twitter-shaped; Result the product is closer to a Doola-shaped reseller of regulated rails with an AI skin. That is the gap I cannot close from the founder's audience alone.

Single-sided SaaS where each founder customer is economically independent of the next — that is the failure mechanism that pushes companies out of the upside math I underwrite, because the (N-1) user does not get more value from the Nth signup. Result's dossier offers no mechanic that breaks this: a founder using Result to incorporate and run support agents gets nothing from another founder doing the same. Compare to Stack Overflow (2008), where the Nth developer's answer made every prior reader more valuable; nothing in the Result description hints at a directory, a shared agent marketplace, a founder-to-founder graph, or anything that turns supply into demand. The "10,000x upside" math from Knowledge Project #18 needs network compounding to even be legible, and Result's bundle is operationally a parallel set of one-to-one workflows.

Savio Martin shipping Slickr (588 stars) and Codehouse (961 stars) before YC is the Ivan-Zhao-rebuilding-productivity-tools-since-adolescence pattern in miniature — specific knowledge as builder, not as MBA market-mapper. Over 18 months that compounds: the CTO who shipped multiple consumer products at scale will iterate Result's UX faster than a Doola PM working through Jira. But specific knowledge in *consumer builder tools* is not specific knowledge in *founder-ops infrastructure*; the incorporation, banking, and compliance surfaces want a different muscle, and Aaryan's 90nine.in journalism background plus a TreeHacks AI-agent prize does not close that gap. The founder-product-market fit reads strong on the AI-agent layer and thin on the regulated-rail layer.

Twitter pivoted out of Odeo's podcasting failure into messaging because the consumer-media market had room for the swing. Result has comparable pivot room: founder-ops is platform-shaped, and if the incorporation bundle gets crushed by Mercury or Doola adding agents, Savio can collapse Result down to "AI ops agent for solo founders" without refinancing — that pivot fits inside the original $500K-ish pre-seed envelope because the agent layer is the code-leverage piece, and the rest can be deprecated. Where Result loses points against the Notion analogy on this axis is that the bundle as currently described requires three regulated partners to even ship V1, and that capital intensity narrows the pivot window before product-market fit lands.

The counterfactual on the founder trio: for Result to score a full HIGH on this dimension, I would need Aaryan to drop the Summer 2026 Coinbase Crypternship the dossier names from freepressjournal.in and 90nine.in to be in maintenance mode, because two 18-year-olds bundling regulated rails on a 2-person team cannot also be moonlighting at Coinbase Consumer Product Group during the YC batch. The integrity reads clean, the energy reads off the charts, and the intelligence shows in the shipped artifacts — but the 10-year-partner test from Knowledge Project #18 wants founder bandwidth concentration the dossier currently contradicts. That is fixable; it is also a real signal right now.

My 36-month forecast on Result: the incorporation-and-banking bundle gets out-shipped by Mercury or Doola adding native AI ops agents inside 18 months, Result narrows to an AI-agent layer for solo and pre-incorporation founders, lands at $500K–$2M ARR with Savio's audience driving distribution, and ends up either acqui-hired by Mercury for Savio specifically or limping as a profitable lifestyle tool. The mechanism producing that path is the high/low signature: Savio's media leverage is sufficient to attract the first wave of founders, but the product's network mechanic is too weak to compound them, so growth tops out at the size of the CTO's distribution funnel.

The bull case that stings: this is the Replit (April 2023) shape one cycle earlier — Amjad Masad was a self-taught code-environment obsessive who looked like a Codehouse-style builder before the AI cycle made his platform a unicorn, and Aaron Epstein at YC is sharp enough that the Spring 2026 batch may already encode an AI-agent-platform thesis I am underweighting. If Result becomes the default place a YC W26 or S26 founder lands their first $1 of revenue, the AngelList-style operator-leverage flywheel kicks in and the network mechanic I cannot find in the dossier emerges through batch-cohort effects. The verifiable condition that flips my verdict: Result ships an open-source agent runtime — the Codehouse-pattern applied to founder ops — that hits 1,000+ GitHub stars inside 12 months and is being forked by founders outside YC. That would convert Result from permissioned-rail-reseller to code-leverage-native, and I would write the check at the next round.
