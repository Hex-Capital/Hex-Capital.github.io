# Refortifai -- Paul Graham Evaluation

Refortifai is a real schlep wrapped around the wrong founders for that schlep, and the giveaway is that refortif.ai returned HTTP 403 when the dossier was pulled. The schlep is genuine -- EE Times (Apr 2026) calls analog design "historically resistant to automation because of reliance on engineering intuition," and that is exactly the kind of unpleasant zone I like. But Refortifai's CTO Atman Kar's RTL design work at Texas Instruments and Tenstorrent is *digital* RTL, not analog, and the dossier's own risk section flags "no public documentation of CEO Sayan Mitra's analog-circuit or semiconductor background." A team aimed at analog with a digital-RTL CTO and an undocumented CEO is not the **Coinbase** (2012) pattern; it is the inverse.

Brian Armstrong shipped a working Android Bitcoin wallet between the day **Coinbase** (2012) was rejected and the day it was readmitted, and that single artifact is what an animal looks like pre-funding. Refortifai shows none of that signal. The CEO Sayan Mitra has no public GitHub, no Twitter, no documented prior work; the COO Rithik Jain has no GitHub; the CTO has 11 repos and 11 followers with a top repo at 15 stars (ERC-BPGC/Trotbot, which is a college robotics repo, not an analog-EDA hack). Refortifai's website returns 403. The pre-funding artifact filter that flipped me on **Dropbox** (2007) -- Drew Houston's demo video on Hacker News -- has no counterpart here. There is no demo, no benchmark, no released RL environment, no paper, no waitlist number. Animals leave tracks; Refortifai's track is a 403.

Drew Houston of **Dropbox** (2007) kept forgetting his USB stick at MIT before he wrote a line of code; Refortifai's CTO Atman Kar spent his career writing digital RTL at TI and interning on CPU RTL at Tenstorrent, which is one priesthood over from analog. Analog and digital are different animals -- analog is intuition, hand-tuned bias points, parasitic capacitance; digital is synthesis flows and place-and-route. Refortifai's organic-pain story would be tight if the product were a digital RTL copilot. For analog, the founder-pain origin is asserted by category, not by biography. The dossier offers nothing showing any Refortifai founder personally suffered an analog tape-out.

Analog automation is a schlep zone -- and that is the single dimension where Refortifai earns real credit. Synopsys and Cadence have shipped agentic features into the digital flow and treated analog as the hard tail; Astrus took the narrow slice (layout); Cognichip went foundation-model. The full "build + debug + automate" loop for analog circuits is the part of the room nobody wants to mop, which is precisely the "Schlep Blindness" (January 2012) shape. If Refortifai's three founders were the right animals for this schlep, I would lean in. The schlep is the asset; what I cannot find in the dossier is anyone hardened enough to grind on it for seven years.

Cognichip raised $60M Series A from Seligman Ventures with Intel CEO Lip-Bu Tan on the board and is already engaged with 30+ semiconductor companies; Astrus took $8M USD from Khosla; Quilter took $10M Series A from Benchmark. Refortifai walks into a category where the schlep has been named, the capital has been allocated, and the customer rolodexes have been opened by people with bigger reputations. The counterfactual question -- what would have to be true for Refortifai's hacker-genealogy score to flip a tier -- is something like: Atman Kar would need to release an open-source analog RL environment that picks up a few hundred GitHub stars from the chip-design community before Demo Day. A 15-star college robotics repo does not pin Refortifai near the **Reddit** (2005) Swartz/RSS rung.

Refortifai is a three-person team where two of the three (Atman and Rithik) share NC State graduate-school connections, which is a real but thin chemistry signal -- not the **Airbnb** (2009) Chesky/Gebbia/Blecharczyk RISD-plus-Harvard-engineer trio, and not the **Reddit** (2005) dorm-roommate bond. Sayan Mitra's relationship to the other two is undocumented. Three founders is the right shape; the bonding evidence is thin but neutral.

My 36-month forecast for Refortifai: pivots within 18 months to a digital RTL copilot adjacent to Atman Kar's actual TI/Tenstorrent experience, or shuts down; does not cross $1M ARR in analog while Cognichip's 30-customer pipeline matures. The mechanism is straightforward -- analog requires a depth of intuition that the dossier shows no Refortifai founder possessing, and the CTO's real edge is on the digital side where the schlep is already being eaten by Cadence.AI.

The strongest case against my pass goes like this: Cognichip is a foundation-model bet across digital/analog/mixed-signal, Astrus is narrowly layout, and the agentic "build + debug" loop for analog circuits is genuinely uncovered -- meaning Refortifai is in the schlep zone three better-funded competitors are routing around. **Airbnb** (2009) is the precedent for funding a team whose idea I had mentally rejected, and the override there was the cereal-box stunt. Refortifai's dossier contains no cereal-box equivalent -- no signed LOI, no paying design house, no Hacker News thread with an analog circuit auto-generated and verified. I would flip to invest if Atman Kar publishes a reproducible RL benchmark for a non-trivial analog block (op-amp, bandgap, PLL) with measurable improvement over a Cadence Virtuoso baseline before YC Demo Day. Until then, no artifact, no animal, no check.
